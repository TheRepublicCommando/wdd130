Overview

Our Purpose

The sole purpose of the Happy Dragon is to provide a nice, safe space for people to come hangout and/or study at. We aswell strive to also share our many delicous foods, snacks, and of course RootBeer.

Audience

Are you having a hard time studying for that test or exam coming up and dont have a good place to study at? Then this place is for you! Need a meeting place for you DnD group? We can do that. Curious about all the different kinds of rootbeer there are out there? We got you. Just need a little snack? We have them. Just stop on by the Happy Dragon, and we'll see what we can do for you!

Home page

Welcome to the website of the Happy Dragon! We are so glad you could make it. If you didn’t already know, The Happy Dragon is a mix of a Cafe style and bar style. We have a variety of foods and baked goods for you to enjoy along with an assortment of Root Beers. There is no Alcohol aloud. We strive to make the Happy Dragon a family friendly place and also a safe space to hangout at.

We have noticed over the years that there aren't exactly a lot of safe places for people to hangout/study at. Sure there's the public library for studying, but that’s not a good place to hangout and have fun. Then there’s home, but sometimes home is, how should I say this, complicated for some so not a great place to hangout/study at for some. To fight this, we encourage students to come hangout here where they are most welcome to be.

Need somewhere to work/study on a paper, you are most welcome. Need a tavern style for your D&D campaign to help set the mood for your party? COME ON OVER! Curious about some of our Root Beers? Don’t be shy, if you want we can see what kind of root beer would best suit you, maybe even make a special “concoction” if you dare.

If you have any questions please let us know.

Events

Here at the Happy Dragon, we do like to put on some special events, deals, and classes.

Our more regular events like D&D night where on every 3rd Friday of the month you can bring your entire D&D group and we will have a special treat for your group, and if needs be, act out as the Barkeeps.

Another regular event we have is a Root Beer making class, where as you may have guessed, learn how to make Root Beer. Going over the Different ways of making it, how creative you can get with it, and what to stay away from when making Root Beer.

You can sign up here.
