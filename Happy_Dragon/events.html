Here at the Happy Dragon, we do like to put on some special events, deals, and classes. Our more regular events like D&D night where on every 3rd Friday of the month you can bring your entire D&D group and we will have a special treat for your group, and if needs be, act out as the Barkeeps. Another regular event we have is a Root Beer making class, where as you may have guessed, learn how to make Root Beer. Going over the Different ways of making it, how creative you can get with it, and what to stay away from when making Root Beer. You can sign up here. We will of course have some special events for the Holidays, especially Christmas. Below is a list of events we know is happening. Also some mystery events we want to keep a surprise for now.

D&D night

Every Friday and Saturday night is D&D night. You can call in ahead of time and reserve a table for your group and just have a night of fun.

Dragon Studies

Monday-Thursday afterschool is study time. You are more than welcome to swing by and hangout. If you need any help with your work feel free to ask. We do try to have most of the books that you guys would need in school

Rootbeer making Classes

We do have Rootbeer making classes during the summer so if you want to see how to make your own Rootbeer and what to stay away from, feel free to ask Kaleb. More info below.
